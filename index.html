I’m a mixed-methods UX Researcher currently working at GreatSchools. My about page has more details :)

Early 2025

Simplifying School Profile Navigation

Conducted unmoderated interviews with open-ended and hybrid card sorts to understand how users naturally categorize school information, followed by a tree test to validate findings and refine the final taxonomy of the redesigned school profile page on GreatSchools.org.



Late 2024

Clarifying School Ratings

Moderated user interviews to understand how parents look up their children's K-12 school and how they discover potential schools, followed by progressive discolsure exercise to understand when what info is needed, followed by usability testing of the re-designed hero and school rating section.
