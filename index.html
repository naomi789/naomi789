Video Annotation Tool

Simplifying data labeling for machine learning

Data from gyroscopes and accelerometers often has multiple axises and can be difficult to read, but having a video that shows the sensor does not always make annotators more accurate or efficient. I designed a study to investigate why this might be the case, ran 60 users through the study, and wrote scripts to analyze quantitative data about how users used our Video Annotation Tool (VAT) and their annotations.
